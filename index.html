Career mentorship

Guiding students toward the right path

Most students in Bhawani Mandi and nearby villages lack guidance on career paths. They choose random careers or drop out. Mismatch between industry needs & student skills leads to unemployment. We're building a mentorship ecosystem where IIT/IIM/AIIMS alumni guide students on education, skills, and real career options.

About us
Built by people who grew up in towns like yours

We are a group of people from diverse backgrounds — graduated from IITs, IIMs, AIIMS, NLUs — united by a shared vision of creating meaningful impact and contributing back to our roots.

Saarthi uses technology — AI matching, WhatsApp automation, and data-driven insights — to solve a deeply human problem: connecting people who need work with people who need workers.

We serve: high-intent students who lack resources, capable youth who need motivation, and those unaware of opportunities due to lack of exposure.
